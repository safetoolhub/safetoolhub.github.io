Why SafeToolHub?

In an era where every application demands constant internet connectivity and uploads your personal data to 'the cloud' for analytics or AI training, we decided to take a different path.

SafeToolHub is dedicated to building robust, beautiful, and completely offline applications. Our tools are free and open-source under the GPLv3 license—they don't even contain networking code. Your freedom and privacy are non-negotiable.

⚠️ Windows SmartScreen Warning

Since this is an indie open-source app, Windows might warn you when running the installer.

To install it, you can either:

Option 1: Right-click the downloaded file > Properties > Check 'Unblock' > OK. Show where

Option 2: When the blue SmartScreen appears, click 'More info' and then 'Run anyway'. Show where

⚠️ macOS App Blocked Warning

You can force the app to open by authorizing it directly from your Mac's settings.

1. Try to double-click the blocked file so the system registers the attempt, then close the warning window.

2. Click the Apple menu in the top-left corner and select System Settings (or System Preferences on older versions).

3. Go to Privacy & Security and scroll down to the Security section.

4. You'll see a message indicating the file was blocked; click Open Anyway and enter your Mac password to confirm. Show where
